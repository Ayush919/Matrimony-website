Why people trust and feel safe on Truely Marry

We protect your privacy, strive to let only genuine people in, and help to keep you safe.

We are not the typical matrimonial site. We are not a casual dating app, either.

We are different in that we believe:

we believePeople are not 2-minute noodles

Noodles may be cooked in two minutes, and so may a profile on other matchmaking sites and apps. Not here. Our thoughtful, in-depth profile questionnaire shows that a prospect is earnest, and helps you truly get to know someone.

we believeQuality matters

No riff-raff or “time pass” seekers here. No database bloated with fake profiles, either. We manually screen each profile and ruthlessly boot suspect users. By raising the bar, we strive to ensure that you see only genuine profiles of people who are serious about finding a match.

we believe People marry when they are ready

People at different stages in their lives and careers have different relationship goals and time frames for marriage. We recognize that. We match people according to their life stages and desired time frames too. No more ambiguity.

we believe In matching people, not “bio data”

We’re not merely classified ads gone online. Yet, no AI BS here, either. Just BI (basic intelligence). We bring out the person behind a profile and also what you truly want in a partner. The result: Truer matches. Better yet, your preferences stay hidden. So, no one can game them and you don’t have to worry about being judged.

we believe Private info should stay private

Most other sites let random visitors view your name and/or photos, as bait for new users. We don’t. We don’t expose unsuspecting users’ profiles to search engines, either. Your profile and photos are displayed by default only to registered users with verified phone numbers and who also pass your match filters.

we believeFinding a match is just the beginning

We don’t just find you a match. We help you set the foundation for a successful, lasting relationship.
